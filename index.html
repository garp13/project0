Frequently Asked Questions

What fees do I get charged?

Listing your products on Zilingo is always
free. Based on
product categories, we do
charge a commission - from 10% to
20% - on
the value of orders placed on Zilingo. Also,
sellers
pay pick-up and delivery charges for
every order. These
charges are determined
by our logistics partners on the basis
of
weight, size, and destination.

How do I stand out on Zilingo?

Its pretty simple. Follow these 5 cardinal
rules:

#1 Sell products that people want (AKA you
need to follow
trends)

#2 Good price vs. quality beats all

#3 God-like images

#4 Detailed and accurate names

#5 Lots of descriptions

How do I create a store on Zilingo

All you need is to create an account and add
at least 5 SKUs.
Once we confirm the details
are in order, you can start selling!

How do I get paid?

All payments are made to a bank account
specified by you.
Payment details are
collected during the onboarding process.
